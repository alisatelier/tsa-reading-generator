KNOWLEDGE TYPE: pip_suits
PURPOSE:
- Provide the core archetype for each suit.
- When interpreting a pip card, combine:
  (1) Suit archetype + (2) Pip number archetype (+ reversal if reversed).

SUIT: Sparks
ARCHEtype NAME: The Soul
ASSOCIATIONS:
- Creativity
- Ambition
- Dreams
- Passion projects
- Dharma
- The work that confirms our place in the world
INTERPRETATION NOTES:
- Sparks speaks to creative life force, inspiration, purpose, and forward drive.
- When Sparks is challenging, it can show burnout, impatience, ego, or chasing validation instead of true calling.

SUIT: Tears
ARCHETYPE NAME: The Heart
ASSOCIATIONS:
- Emotions
- Connection
- Psychic gifts
- Clairsentience
- The feeling of our inner knowing
- The cosmic cords that connect us all
INTERPRETATION NOTES:
- Tears speaks to emotional truth, intimacy, sensitivity, and what you feel beneath words.
- When Tears is challenging, it can show overwhelm, projection, attachment, or outsourcing emotional safety.

SUIT: Soil
ARCHETYPE NAME: The Hands
ASSOCIATIONS:
- Assets
- The self
- Harvest
- Tangible returns
- Material gains
- Growth
- What you can hold in two hands
INTERPRETATION NOTES:
- Soil speaks to body, resources, money, stability, time, and what becomes real through effort.
- When Soil is challenging, it can show scarcity, overwork, hoarding, stagnation, or misaligned priorities.

SUIT: Whispers
ARCHETYPE NAME: The Mind
ASSOCIATIONS:
- Intellect
- Interactions
- Inner journeys
- Communication
- Intended messages
- Conflict (internal and with others)
INTERPRETATION NOTES:
- Whispers speaks to thoughts, language, perception, and the stories shaping your choices.
- When Whispers is challenging, it can show rumination, miscommunication, avoidance, defensiveness, or mental spirals.
