CARD ID: 35
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): Four of Tears
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not replace TSA name): Four of Cups
SUIT: Tears
PIP: Four

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Four of Tears".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Tears: emotional reality and connection.
2) Add Four (Manifestation): lead yourself to your dreams.
3) Translate: emotional stability and boundaries create space for deeper love; you can choose the feeling you cultivate.
4) Practical step: choose one steady heart practice (journaling, therapy, prayer, honest conversation, rest).

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Tears domain.
2) Use Four (Reversed): illusion; your dreams are out of touch.
3) Name the friction: idealizing someone/something, numbness, ignoring what’s offered, or craving a fantasy instead of a real bond.
4) Correction: return to the present—name what is available now and choose one grounded action that supports it.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: emotional grounding; choosing steadiness; heart boundaries; calm receptivity.
- Challenging: dissatisfaction; emotional withdrawal; fantasy bonding; missing what’s real.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- What is my heart overlooking because I’m focused on what I wish were true?
