HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Soil: exchange, resources, tangible support, stability.
2) Add Six (Epiphany): you are in the flow.
3) Translate: balance in giving/receiving—support arrives, resources circulate, generosity becomes sustainable.
4) Practical step: correct the exchange—ask for help, offer help, or renegotiate fairness.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Soil domain.
2) Use Six (Reversed): conceal; others may seek your knowing.
3) Name the friction: strings attached, unequal exchange, hidden expectations, or someone taking more than they give.
4) Correction: clarify terms; set boundaries; document agreements; choose reciprocal relationships.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: fair exchange; help received; sustainable generosity; resources flowing.
- Challenging: imbalance; transactional dynamics; hidden strings; over-giving or under-receiving.
