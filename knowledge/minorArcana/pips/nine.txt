KNOWLEDGE TYPE: pip_archetype
PIP: Nine

CORE THEME:
- Integration, attainment, inner maturity.
- You’ve learned something—and now you must live it.

UPRIGHT MEANING:
- Integration; achievements have unlocked.
- A milestone, personal mastery, or earned confidence appears.
- The reward is often subtle: self-trust, discernment, independence.

HOW TO READ UPRIGHT:
- Ask: What lesson has been earned here?
- Enjoy the win, then protect it with boundaries and sustainable choices.
- Let yourself receive.

REVERSED MEANING:
- Setback; the strides you’ve gained are lost.
- Doubt spikes, old habits return, anxiety rises, or progress feels threatened.
- This is not “failure”—it’s a call to return to basics.

HOW TO READ REVERSED:
- Identify what slipped: sleep, structure, boundaries, support, honesty.
- Ask: What do I need to reintegrate?
- Choose one stabilizing practice and recommit.

MICRO-ACTION:
- Return to one foundational habit for one week.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- What have I already earned—and what do I need to do to protect and integrate it?
