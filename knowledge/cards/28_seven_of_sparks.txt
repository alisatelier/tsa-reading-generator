CARD ID: 28
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): Seven of Sparks
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not replace TSA name): Seven of Wands
SUIT: Sparks
PIP: Seven

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Seven of Sparks".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Sparks: ambition, creative purpose, dharma work.
2) Add Seven (Resilience): adversity must be overcome.
3) Translate: your work matters enough to be tested; you’re asked to stand for your vision.
4) Practical step: choose one boundary or defense of your time/energy and hold it consistently.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Sparks domain.
2) Use Seven (Reversed): resistance; adversity must be overcome.
3) Name the friction: procrastination, fear of conflict, giving up too early, or self-sabotage.
4) Correction: reduce the battle—choose the simplest winning strategy (small daily effort + one protected block of time).

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: courage under pressure; defending your calling; perseverance.
- Challenging: avoidance; wavering; letting external noise steer your choices.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- What am I unwilling to protect that I actually need to defend for my dream to live?
