NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Nine of Soil".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Soil: tangible returns, assets, self-worth, stability.
2) Add Nine (Integration): achievements have unlocked.
3) Translate: earned security, independence, and embodied confidence—your work has produced real value.
4) Practical step: enjoy the harvest and protect it with wise boundaries and sustainable routines.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Soil domain.
2) Use Nine (Reversed): setback; the strides you’ve gained are lost.
3) Name the friction: feeling financially/physically off-track, self-worth dips, or a routine slipping.
4) Correction: return to basics—budget, body care, simple structure, and one reliable habit.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: independence; tangible success; self-trust; stability you can feel.
- Challenging: insecurity; overspending/overworking; self-worth tied to output; losing rhythm.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- What part of my stability is already earned, and what boundary will protect it?
