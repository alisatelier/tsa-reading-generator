CARD ID: 16
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): The Fortress
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not name it in the reading): The Tower
ELEMENT: Fire

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "The Fortress" (never call it "The Tower").
- You may use traditional Tower symbolism internally, but the public-facing name must remain "The Fortress".

CORE MEANING (UPRIGHT SUMMARY):
- A sudden truth that breaks illusion.
- A disruption that frees you from a structure that can’t hold.
- The fall of a false foundation; reality insisting on honesty.
- A necessary clearing so something truer can be rebuilt.

UPRIGHT (ALI BIRD VOICE):
When attitudes of decadence adorn our internal castles, the streets of our humility can be left in foul states. If all attention is placed on the glimmer and glitz of our disposition, the foundations of our character can become compromised. Mirrors reflect what we seek to portray, but even a hairline crack can cast an altered view. Beware of such brittle echoes, for no shattered glass bears a good omen.

REVERSED (SUMMARY):
- Crisis averted; the collapse slows or becomes manageable.
- Merit maintained; choosing repair before rupture.
- Clinging to the status quo; avoiding an inevitable truth.

REVERSED (ALI BIRD VOICE):
When The Fortress appears reversed, the cracks may be visible — but the fall is not inevitable. This can be a moment of repair: humility returns, truth is spoken, and the foundation is reinforced before it gives way. It can also be a warning: avoiding the truth doesn’t remove it; it only delays the reckoning. Choose honesty early. A small, courageous correction now can prevent a larger collapse later.

WHEN THIS CARD SHOWS UP:
- Something is being revealed: a truth, a misalignment, a weak foundation.
- A false narrative is losing power.
- The lesson is honesty and humility — rebuilding on what is real.

PRACTICAL GUIDANCE:
- Ask: “What is no longer structurally sound in my life?”
- Choose one truth to acknowledge plainly (to yourself first, then others if needed).
- Stabilize your nervous system: rest, hydration, and steady support while you rebuild.

KEYWORDS / TAGS:
- truth, disruption, revelation, collapse, awakening, humility, reset, rebuilding, liberation

ALIAS / SEARCH HELP (for retrieval only):
- The Tower, sudden change, revelation, breakdown breakthrough, collapse, truth revealed
