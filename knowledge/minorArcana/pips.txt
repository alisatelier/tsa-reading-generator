KNOWLEDGE TYPE: pip_numbers
PURPOSE:
- Provide the core archetype for pip numbers One through Ten.
- When interpreting a pip card, combine:
  (1) Suit archetype + (2) Pip number archetype (upright or reversed).

UPRIGHT PIPS:

ONE
- Beginnings.
- The potential is infinite.

TWO
- Choices.
- Intuition will guide the way.

THREE
- Growth.
- One must be raised to rise.

FOUR
- Manifestation.
- Lead yourself to your dreams.

FIVE
- Loss.
- Finding gratitude to heal.

SIX
- Epiphany.
- You are in the flow.

SEVEN
- Resilience.
- Adversity must be overcome.

EIGHT
- Transcendence.
- Greater heights await.

NINE
- Integration.
- Achievements have unlocked.

TEN
- Completion.
- You have arrived.

REVERSED PIPS:

ONE (REVERSED)
- Hesitation.
- The journey must begin.

TWO (REVERSED)
- Indecision.
- Blocking the path’s potential.

THREE (REVERSED)
- Agendas.
- A hidden cost to expand.

FOUR (REVERSED)
- Illusion.
- Your dreams are out of touch.

FIVE (REVERSED)
- Shock.
- The fortress is crumbling.

SIX (REVERSED)
- Conceal.
- Others may seek your knowing.

SEVEN (REVERSED)
- Resistance.
- Adversity must be overcome.

EIGHT (REVERSED)
- Stagnation.
- You have reached a plateau.

NINE (REVERSED)
- Setback.
- The strides you’ve gained are lost.

TEN (REVERSED)
- Completion.
- But not in the way you hoped.
