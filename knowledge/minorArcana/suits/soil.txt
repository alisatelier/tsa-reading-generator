KNOWLEDGE TYPE: suit_archetype
SUIT: Soil
ALIAS (reference only): Pentacles

Soil = The Hands
- Assets, the self, harvest, tangible returns, material gains, embodied growth.
- What you can hold in two hands: time, money, health, home, stability.

Reading guidance:
- Upright: practicality, stability, steady progress, support structures.
- Reversed: scarcity mindset, stagnation, misaligned priorities, insecurity.

Tone rules:
- Never speak in absolutes.
- Offer agency and a practical next step.
