CARD ID: 57
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): Six of Whispers
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not replace TSA name): Six of Swords
SUIT: Whispers
PIP: Six

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Six of Whispers".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Whispers: mind, perception, communication, internal journeys.
2) Add Six (Epiphany): you are in the flow.
3) Translate: mental relief; moving away from chaos; choosing calmer waters through clearer thinking.
4) Practical step: take one step away from conflict—change the environment, simplify decisions, seek clarity.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Whispers domain.
2) Use Six (Reversed): conceal; others may seek your knowing.
3) Name the friction: keeping plans private, needing discretion, or someone probing for information they haven’t earned.
4) Correction: protect sensitive thoughts; communicate selectively; document and verify before sharing.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: moving on; mental clarity; calmer communication; transition.
- Challenging: gossip risk; information leaks; needing discretion; trust issues.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- What thought pattern am I ready to leave behind so my mind can move into calmer waters?
