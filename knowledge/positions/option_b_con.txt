POSITION KEY: option_b_con
POSITION NAME: Option B — Con

Meaning:
- The cost, risk, or trade-off of choosing Option B.
- What Option B delays, complicates, or requires patience for.
- The “shadow” of B: what you’ll need to manage if you pick it.

How to read this position:
- Identify the downside clearly and calmly.
- Name the most likely friction point (where you’ll feel it).
- Offer a simple plan to address it (one boundary, one action, or one support).

Helpful angles:
- What is the opportunity cost of B (what you might miss or postpone)?
- What could feel limiting?
- What pattern might repeat if you choose B unconsciously?

Rules:
- No absolutes. No doom.
- Always offer one actionable way to reduce the downside.
