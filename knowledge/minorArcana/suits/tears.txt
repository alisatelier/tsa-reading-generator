SUIT: Tears
ALIAS (reference only): Cups

Tears = The Heart
- Emotions, connection, psychic gifts, clairsentience.
- The feeling of inner knowing. The cords that connect us all.

Reading guidance:
- Upright: intimacy, empathy, emotional honesty, healing, receptivity.
- Reversed: emotional fog, over-attachment, avoidance, numbness, misattunement.

Tone rules:
- Never speak in absolutes.
- Offer agency and a practical next step.
