POSITION KEY: option_a_con
POSITION NAME: Option A — Con

Meaning:
- The cost, risk, or trade-off of choosing Option A.
- What Option A asks you to tolerate, release, or work through.
- The “shadow” of A: what could complicate things if not handled well.

How to read this position:
- Name the trade-off without dramatizing it.
- Offer a prevention or mitigation strategy (a boundary, plan, or support).
- Frame it as information—not a warning label.

Helpful angles:
- What might you lose (time, flexibility, comfort, closeness, certainty)?
- What becomes harder to maintain?
- What could create resentment if you ignore it?

Rules:
- No fear language. No absolutes.
- End this section with one practical way to reduce the downside.
