POSITION KEY: block
POSITION NAME: Block

Meaning:
- The main friction point slowing progress (internal or external).
- A constraint, fear, pattern, belief, or practical obstacle.
- What must be acknowledged honestly (without shame) to move forward.

How to interpret:
- Name the block clearly.
- Distinguish between:
  - internal blocks (fear, avoidance, perfectionism, old stories)
  - external blocks (time, money, logistics, other peopleâ€™s limits)

Tone rules:
- Blocks are information, not failure.
- Avoid blame and avoid catastrophizing.
