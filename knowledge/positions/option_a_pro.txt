POSITION KEY: option_a_pro
POSITION NAME: Option A — Pro

Meaning:
- The strongest benefit of choosing Option A.
- What Option A makes easier, smoother, or more aligned.
- The “tailwind” you can expect if you commit to A.

How to read this position:
- Name the most practical upside first (what improves in real life).
- Then name the emotional/spiritual upside (what it supports inside you).
- Be specific about where the benefit shows up (time, energy, money, relationships, confidence, peace).

Helpful angles:
- What becomes simpler?
- What tension is reduced?
- What door opens because of A?

Rules:
- Do not claim certainty. Speak in possibilities.
- Keep it grounded: “If you choose A, you may notice…”
