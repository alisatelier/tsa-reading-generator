POSITION KEY: goal
POSITION NAME: Goal

Meaning:
- What you’re truly aiming for (the deeper desire beneath the surface).
- The most aligned version of the outcome.
- What success looks like in a way that fits your values and capacity.

How to interpret:
- Name the “real goal,” not just the surface want.
- Clarify what the goal requires (commitment, time, courage, skill, support).

Tone rules:
- Encourage clarity and self-honesty.
- Avoid pressure or perfectionism.
