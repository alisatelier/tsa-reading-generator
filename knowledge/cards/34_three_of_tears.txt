CARD ID: 34
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): Three of Tears
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not replace TSA name): Three of Cups
SUIT: Tears
PIP: Three

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Three of Tears".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Tears: connection, feelings, intuitive bonds.
2) Add Three (Growth): one must be raised to rise.
3) Translate: emotional growth through community—friendship, support, shared joy, vulnerability in a safe circle.
4) Practical step: strengthen your support network—one reach-out, one invite, one honest share.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Tears domain.
2) Use Three (Reversed): agendas; a hidden cost to expand.
3) Name the friction: social dynamics, jealousy, triangulation, people-pleasing, or intimacy offered with conditions.
4) Correction: choose emotional integrity—prioritize safe relationships and clarify expectations.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: community support; celebration; heartening connection; shared healing.
- Challenging: drama; hidden motives; feeling excluded; connection that costs your peace.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- Who helps me rise—and what does my heart need from my community right now?
