POSITION KEY: option_b_pro
POSITION NAME: Option B — Pro

Meaning:
- The strongest benefit of choosing Option B.
- What Option B protects, preserves, or strengthens.
- The “tailwind” you can expect if you commit to B.

How to read this position:
- Identify what B supports that A does not.
- Include both external results (what changes around you) and internal results (what changes within you).
- Make it situational and specific.

Helpful angles:
- What becomes more sustainable?
- What do you get to keep?
- What part of you relaxes or comes alive?

Rules:
- Speak in possibilities and agency (“If you choose B…”).
- Keep it balanced and practical.
