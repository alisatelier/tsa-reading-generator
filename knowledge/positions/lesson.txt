POSITION KEY: lesson
POSITION NAME: Lesson

Meaning:
- What this goal is teaching you about yourself.
- The growth edge you’re building (patience, courage, discernment, boundaries, trust, discipline).
- How to integrate the learning so progress becomes sustainable.

How to interpret:
- Name the “becoming” that is happening alongside the doing.
- Offer a reflection that helps the user embody the lesson.

Tone rules:
- Encouraging and compassionate.
- Keep it actionable: a lesson should lead to a practice or mindset.
