KNOWLEDGE TYPE: suit_archetype
SUIT: Sparks
ALIAS (reference only): Wands

Sparks = The Soul
- Creativity, ambition, dreams, passion projects, dharma.
- The work that confirms our place in the world.
- When Sparks shows up, ask: What wants to be created? Where is the fire asking for devotion?

Reading guidance:
- Upright: momentum, inspiration, courage, purposeful action.
- Reversed: burnout, scattered energy, avoidance, fear of visibility, false starts.

Tone rules:
- Never speak in absolutes.
- Offer agency and a practical next step.
