CARD ID: 33
CANONICAL TSA NAME (always use this name in the reading): Two of Tears
RWS EQUIVALENT (reference only; do not replace TSA name): Two of Cups
SUIT: Tears
PIP: Two

NAMING RULE:
- In all writing outputs, call this card "Two of Tears".

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (UPRIGHT):
1) Start with Tears: connection and emotional truth.
2) Add Two (Choices): intuition will guide the way.
3) Translate: a relationship choice is present—mutuality, bonding, reconciliation, or a new connection.
4) Practical step: choose honest reciprocity—say what you feel, ask what you need, listen for alignment.

HOW TO READ THIS CARD (REVERSED):
1) Keep Tears domain.
2) Use Two (Reversed): indecision; blocking the path’s potential.
3) Name the friction: mixed signals, fear of commitment, attachment to an unavailable dynamic, or refusing to choose clarity.
4) Correction: ask one direct question; set one boundary; choose the option that honors emotional safety.

QUICK SIGNALS:
- Supportive: mutual connection; emotional harmony; partnership energy; bonding.
- Challenging: imbalance; unclear intentions; hesitation to commit; avoidance of truth.

JOURNAL PROMPT:
- Where am I craving connection, and what would true reciprocity look like?
