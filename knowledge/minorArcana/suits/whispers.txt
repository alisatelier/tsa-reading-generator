SUIT: Whispers
ALIAS (reference only): Swords

Whispers = The Mind
- Intellect, communication, interactions, inner journeys.
- Intended messages. Conflict (internal or with others).

Reading guidance:
- Upright: clarity, truth, discernment, direct communication.
- Reversed: rumination, avoidance, distortion, mental overload, miscommunication.

Tone rules:
- Never speak in absolutes.
- Offer agency and a practical next step.
