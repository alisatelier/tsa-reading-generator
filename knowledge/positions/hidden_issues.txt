POSITION KEY: hidden_issues
POSITION NAME: Hidden Issues

Meaning:
- What is influencing the situation beneath the surface.
- Unspoken needs, fears, assumptions, or pressure.
- The “quiet truth” that becomes clear with honesty and slowing down.

How to interpret:
- Keep it grounded: name dynamics, not accusations.

Tone rules:
- Do not mind-read. Speak in patterns and possibilities.
