POSITION KEY: status
POSITION NAME: Status

Meaning:
- The current reality: what is true right now.
- What resources you have (time, energy, support, skill, money, confidence).
- What is already working (or trying to work), even if imperfect.

How to interpret:
- Describe the situation as it is, without judgment.
- Identify the “lever” that can be influenced first.

Tone rules:
- Grounded and practical.
- No dramatizing; this is a reality check, not a verdict.
